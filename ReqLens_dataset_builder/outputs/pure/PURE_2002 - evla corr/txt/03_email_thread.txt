Email 2:
From: Mike (Project Scientist)
To: Laura (Software Developer), Team
Subject: RE: Clarification on Correlator Control System Integration

Hi Laura,

Good questions. The system must integrate fully within the EVLA M&C infrastructure but maintain isolation of the correlator hardware to ease programmability and maintenance. We expect adherence to IEEE 802.3 Ethernet standards at 100 Mbits/sec or better. Network interfaces should be separated to ensure isolation between components.

Best,
Mike

---

Email 6:
From: Mike (Project Scientist)
To: Laura (Software Developer), Sarah (Security Specialist), Team
Subject: RE: Data Handling and Fault Recovery Questions

Hi Laura,

Yes, the system must spool ancillary monitor data including system health, error messages, and configuration echoes during temporary network communication loss. If a CMIB subsystem fails and does not respond to reboot or self-heal attempts, the system must issue an alert for hardware repair and automatically restart and reconfigure the subsystem.

Best,
Mike
