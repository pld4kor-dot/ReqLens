Hi Mark,

All web-based displays defined by the ASPERA-3 team for science analysis must be password protected until the data is made public. Additionally, the web server hosting these displays must have appropriate password protection to restrict access to authorized ASPERA-3 team members only.

This is to ensure data security and controlled access during the proprietary period.

Tom
